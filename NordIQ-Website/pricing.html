Pricing FAQ

Do you offer trials or pilots?

Yes! For qualified leads (50+ servers, defined timeline, budget authority), we offer 30-day pilots. Deploy on a subset of your servers and see NordIQ prevent real incidents in your environment.

What payment terms do you offer?

Annual subscription, paid upfront. Net 30 payment terms for qualified enterprises. Monthly payment plans available for Enterprise tier ($75K+) at a 15% premium.

Can I switch tiers?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. If you upgrade mid-contract, we'll prorate the difference. If you downgrade, the new pricing takes effect at renewal.

What if I have more than 5,000 servers?

Contact us for volume pricing. We've successfully deployed at scale with custom architectures for large fleets. Pricing becomes more favorable at 5,000+ servers.

What's included in support?

Self-Hosted: Email support, 24-hour response (business days). Managed SaaS: Priority email support, 8-hour response. Enterprise: Dedicated channel, 4-hour response, on-call escalation option.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The price you see is the price you pay. Software updates, API access, and documentation are included. Optional: Professional services ($150-250/hr) if you need custom integration help.

What happens if I cancel?

Annual contracts with 30-day cancellation notice before renewal. If you cancel mid-year, no refund (you've already had the service), but we'll help you export your data and migrate smoothly.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! Multi-year contracts (2-3 years): 10-15% discount. Startups (<50 employees, Series A or earlier): 20% discount. Non-profits and education: 30% discount. Contact us for details.
